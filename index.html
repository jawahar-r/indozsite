What are we all about?

Indoz Rhythms was formed in 2014 by a group of people who were interested in forming a Kerala Chenda group to promote the culture. The objective is to foster the Chendamelam, a traditional art of Kerala to the people of Kerala (or Indian) origin, among the people in Australia, nurture goodwill and understanding between people of India and Australia through education and cultural exchanges.

Why are we different?

Indoz Rhythms comprises of passionate chenda players from diverse backgrounds. We are a team that has been playing Chenda all over Sydney and beyond for the past 10 years. We read the audience and engage them in the best way - whether it is traditional melam or Shingari.
